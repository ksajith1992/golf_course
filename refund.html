1. Introduction

At Range Caddie, we strive to provide the best possible experience for our users. This policy outlines the conditions under which membership fees, guest bookings, and special event fees may be refunded or canceled.

2. General Refund & Cancellation Guidelines

  • Membership Fees (Monthly & Annual Subscriptions)
  • Guest & Special Booking Fees
  • Event or Venue Reservations

3. Membership Refunds & Cancellations

3.1 Monthly Memberships

  • Non-refundable once billed.
  • Cancellations take effect from the next billing cycle.

3.2 Annual Memberships

  • Refundable within 7 days of purchase.
  • No prorated refunds after 7 days.

3.3 Auto-Renewal Policy

  • All memberships auto-renew unless canceled 48 hours before renewal.

4. Guest & Special Booking Refunds & Cancellations

4.1 Standard Guest Bookings

  • Full refund if canceled at least 48 hours before booking.
  • 50% refund if canceled within 24-48 hours.
  • No refund if canceled within 24 hours.

4.2 Special Events & Venue Reservations

  • Full refund if canceled at least 7 days before the event.
  • 50% refund if canceled within 3-7 days.
  • No refund if canceled less than 3 days before the event.

5. Refund Processing Time

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
  • Issued to the original payment method.

6. Payment Disputes & Chargebacks

  • Refund requests must be submitted before initiating chargebacks.
  • Unauthorized chargebacks may result in account suspension.

7. Weather-Related Cancellations

  • Full credit for rescheduling if affected by severe weather.
  • Refunds if rescheduling is not possible.

8. No-Show & Late Arrival Policy

  • No-shows are not eligible for refunds.
  • Late arrivals over 15 minutes may result in cancellation without refund.

9. Club & Admin Responsibilities

  • Clubs must manage their refund policies.
  • Admins must approve cancellations within 24 hours.

10. Exceptions & Special Cases

  • Medical emergencies (requires documentation).
  • Technical issues (e.g., double billing).

11. Changes to This Policy

  • Users will be notified of any changes via email or website updates.
